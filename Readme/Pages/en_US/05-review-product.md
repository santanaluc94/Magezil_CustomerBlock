# Block Customer

## Customer Can Review

To allow the customer to evaluate products, follow the steps below:
  - **Step 1:** Magento admin --> Customers --> All Customers --> _Customer_
  - **Step 2:** Account Information --> Customer Can Review
  - **Step 3:** No
